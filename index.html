Belly Button: Secrets?
Microbiologists Point-of-View

Does the bacteria in the human belly button hold the key to producing an artificial beef flavor? For years, microbiologists have tried to determine if beef flavor can be replicated artificially. Now, thanks to research funded by "Probable Beef", they are one step closer to understanding if bacteria from the human belly button can synthesize proteins to replicate the natural beef flavor.

To test the hypothesis, bacterial samples were gathered and analyzed from thousands of people across the country. So, does the bacteria found in the human belly button hold the key to the elusive beef flavor? Check out the data for yourself.
